Welcome to Namazu!

Namazu is Vancouver's newest Japanese-inspired restaurant specializing in fresh local seafood ingredients with catfish as the star of our menu. Our ingredients are sourced from our very own Fisherman's Wharf at Steveston Village!
We are conveniently located at the heart of Downtown Vancouver, beside Bentall Centre making this the perfect venue for your after work gatherings.
